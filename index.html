Sales Forecasting for a Store Network

Utilized Python, statistics, and regression techniques to predict the monetary value of sales that each store in the network would generate over the next 6 months. This model served as a basis for decision-makers to determine investment allocations for store improvements, inventory, personnel, etc., based on the projected financial input. Additionally, the model was connected to a bot on the Telegram messaging app, enabling managers to receive sales forecasts for any store with a simple message, from anywhere and at any time.

  • Git and GitHub
  • Python, Pandas, seaborn
  • Sklearn, Random Forest
  • Render Cloud
  • More

Customer Prioritization for Cross-Selling

Employed a supervised classification algorithm to prioritize a list of customers from an insurance company based on their potential to purchase a second product. The model was integrated into a Google spreadsheet, enabling the sales team to quickly order the list and achieve higher conversion rates. This model achieved at least a 2.5x better ordering compared to a random method and could reach 100% of interested individuals with only 60% of the dataset, resulting in resource savings and improved effectiveness.

  • Python, pandas, matplotlib, seaborn
  • LightGBM Classifier
  • Boruta, Lift Curve
  • More
